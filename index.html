About Me

I am a third year undergraduate student at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur, India, majoring in Manufacturing Science and Engineering. Being a roboticist and tech enthusiast, I like to learn about new technology stuff and apply it myself. Being part of a Autonomous Ground Vehicle Research Group, I have worked on Computer Vision, Control Systems, Embedded Systems. Currently I have been working extensively in deep learning and its application in Self-Driving Cars.
